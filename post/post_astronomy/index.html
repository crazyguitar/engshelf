Dwarf planet 白矮星

Definition:

  1. Orbit around the sun
  2. massive enough for its gravity to crush it into a hydrostatic equilibrium shape
  3. has not cleared the neighborhood of other material around its orbit

Supernova

Definition: A supernova is the explosion of a star. It is the largest explosion that takes place in space.

In Latin, nova means new, referring astronomically to what appears to be a temporary new bright star.

Adding the prefix super- distinguishes supernovae from ordinary novae, which are far less luminous.
